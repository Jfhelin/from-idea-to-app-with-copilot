# Step 3 — Refine It

**The first version is a starting point — now you improve it.**

You do not need to accept the first result.  
This is where the real value comes in.

---

## Start Here

👉 Pick ONE small improvement and try it now.

You can use the examples below — don’t overthink it.

---

## Quick Wins (Try One of These)

- Add a search box  
- Add a filter  
- Change a title  
- Make the layout clearer  

👉 Start small. One change is enough.

---

## Improvement Ideas

Pick any of these — or come up with your own.

---

### Change a label or title

> "Change the heading 'Q3 Revenue' to 'Revenue This Quarter'."

> "Rename the column 'Acct Status' to 'Account Status'."

> "Change the title at the top to 'Sales Performance Overview'."

---

### Add a filter

> "Add a dropdown filter above the customer list so I can filter by account status."

> "Add a way to filter the task board by priority level."

> "Add a search box above the table so I can find a customer by name."

---

### Change the layout

> "Move the four headline numbers into a single row across the top of the page."

> "Make the customer list smaller and give more space to the details."

> "Stack the columns vertically so it looks better on a smaller screen."

---

### Improve clarity

> "Make High priority bold and red, Medium bold and orange, and Low plain text."

> "Add a short explanation under each headline number."

> "Replace short codes with full words like 'Active', 'At Risk', and 'Churned'."

---

### Add something new

> "Add a footer that says 'Last updated: Q3 2025'."

> "Add a short summary at the top."

> "Add a column showing last contact date."

---

## The Pattern

👉 Look → Change → Repeat

That’s it.

---

## If Something Does Not Work

If the result looks wrong, just say:

> "That does not look right. Please go back to how it looked before."

You can always try a different direction.

---

## When You’re Done

You don’t need a perfect app.

Take a moment to:

- Look at what you built  
- Think about how you could use this  

---

## What Just Happened

You started with an idea.

You described it in plain language.

You now have a working application.

👉 That is the core of working with Copilot.
