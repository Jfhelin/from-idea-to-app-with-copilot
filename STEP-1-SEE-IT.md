# Step 1 — See It

**Before you build anything, take a few minutes to see what the end result looks like.**

This step is about setting expectations. You will watch your facilitator build a small app live, from scratch, using only plain English. No typing code. No menus to navigate. Just a conversation.

---

## What You Are About to See

Your facilitator will:

1. Open a chat window — this is where you talk to Copilot
2. Describe a simple app in plain language
3. Let Copilot build it
4. Open the result in a browser

The whole thing takes about two to three minutes.

---

## What You Should Do

For this step, just watch.

You do not need to follow along or click anything yet.

---

## What to Pay Attention To

As you watch, notice three things:

**1. The description is just plain English.**  
There are no special instructions or technical language. The facilitator says what they want the way you would say it to a colleague.

**2. Copilot does the building.**  
You do not see the facilitator writing code or making technical choices. They describe → Copilot builds.

**3. The result is immediately usable.**  
When the build is done, the app opens in a browser and looks like a real tool — not a rough draft.

---

## Example: What the Facilitator Will Say to Copilot

Here is the kind of message your facilitator will type:

> "Build me a simple KPI dashboard for a sales team. It should show total revenue, number of deals closed, average deal size, and a list of the top five customers this quarter. Use realistic sample numbers. Make it clean and easy to read."

That is it. One paragraph. No technical setup before or after.

---

## After the Demo

Once you have seen it, you will know:

- What the finished result looks like
- How little you need to say to get something useful
- What "good" looks like so you can aim for it in the next step

---

## What Happens Next

You are not just an observer. In Step 2, you will do this yourself — and you will have a ready-made list of ideas and prompts to choose from, so you do not need to start from nothing.

When the facilitator says you are ready, move to [Step 2 — Try It](STEP-2-TRY-IT.md).
